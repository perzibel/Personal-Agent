# Image-Related Test Questions

## Sample Queries

1. Do I have a picture of my baby?
2. Show me the latest stroller image.
3. Do I have an image of my ID?
4. Show me the most recent photo of my baby.
5. Find pictures where the stroller appears.
6. Do I have any screenshots saved?
7. Show me the latest screenshot.
8. Find photos that include a passport or ID card.
9. Do I have a picture of a receipt?
10. Show me the newest image from the beach.
11. Find images with a baby bottle.
12. Do I have any photos of the crib?
13. Show me the latest family picture.
14. Find the newest image taken in the car.
15. Do I have any blurry baby pictures?
16. Show me images that contain documents.
17. Find the latest image with a stroller and baby together.
18. Do I have any duplicate-looking screenshots?
19. Show me the newest photo from my phone camera.
20. Find images that might contain personal identification information.

## Notes

* Include both natural-language and object-based queries.
* Test recency-based retrieval such as "latest" and "most recent".
* Test sensitive-document detection such as ID, passport, and receipts.
* Test household and baby-related object recognition such as stroller, crib, and bottle.
* Test screenshot versus camera-photo distinctions where possible.
